Trading Strategies

We Test What the Gurus Teach

Simple, disciplined strategies — tested live every day so you can see exactly what works and what doesn't before spending a dime.

01

15-Minute Opening Range Breakout

The 15 ORB is one of the cleanest setups in day trading. We capture the market's first 15-minute range and trade the breakout — long or short — with defined risk.

  • Define the high & low of the first 15 minutes
  • Wait for a confirmed breakout candle
  • Enter with a tight stop at the opposite side of range
  • Target 1:2 or 1:3 risk-to-reward
  • Perfect for the 9:45–10:45 AM ET window
02

Volume Profile Trading

Volume Profile shows where the most trading activity happened at each price level — revealing key support, resistance, and high-probability reversal zones.

  • Point of Control (POC) — highest volume price
  • Value Area High & Low — 70% of daily volume
  • Low Volume Nodes — fast-moving price levels
  • High Volume Nodes — strong support/resistance
  • Combined with ORB for high-confluence entries
