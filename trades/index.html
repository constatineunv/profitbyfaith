How the Analyzer Works

1NinjaTrader 8 builds the 15-Minute Opening Range (9:30–9:45 ET) and locks ORB High/Low.
2EMA 200 bias filters direction — only long setups above, short below.
3Volume Profile levels (VAH, VAL, POC) confirm confluence — rejections and bounces add score.
4On breakout, the analyzer publishes Entry, Stop, Target and a 0–100 confluence score in real-time.
5If signal conflicts with VP structure, a Conflict Warning fires — trade is flagged, not taken.

Confluence Score Key

80–100 Strong High conviction — multiple VP levels + EMA aligned
60–79 Moderate Solid setup — 2+ factors confirm direction
40–59 Weak Reduced size or skip — conflicting signals
0–39 No Trade Against VP structure — wait for better setup
